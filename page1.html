Dr Naomi Cohn Zentner

Hasidic Nigunim around the Shabbos Table

7:30pm Monday, 4 November 2019
Faust Hall, Oxford Jewish Centre 

21 Richmond Road, Oxford OX1 2JL


Learn about and learn to sing a few Hasidic niguns with the renowned ethnomusicologist, currently Visiting Polonsky Fellow at the Oxford Centre for Hebrew and Jewish Studies, sharing a tish together with wine and nibbles from Stamford Hill. The event is sponsored by the AHRC-funded HASYID project on 'Contemporary Hasidic Yiddish' at UCL.

Attendance is free; pre-registration for non-members is necessary for security reasons at eszterszendroi@gmail.com or connections@ojc-online.org.
